For the Spring 2025 semester due to staffing changes and availability, Comet Cents will not be using Microsoft Bookings for 1-on-1 appointments. To schedule a 1-on-1, please email CometCents@utdallas.edu to schedule a time Monday to Friday from 12 PM to 4 PM.

The Comet Cents Money Management Center provides free education to currently enrolled students on personal financial topics such as saving/budgeting, credit management, student loans, identity theft and financial responsibilities after college.
