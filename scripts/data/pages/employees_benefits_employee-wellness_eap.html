Employee Assistance Program

UT Dallas > Office of Human Resources > Employees > Benefits > Employee Wellness > Employee Assistance Program

Office hours: Monday – Friday 8 a.m. – 5 p.m.

The HR Team is available to assist you via phone, MS Teams, and email.

Walk-in hours are Monday – Friday 10 a.m. – 2 p.m.

The Employee Assistance Program (EAP) is a free and confidential service provided for all UTD employees, their dependents, and household members 24 hours a day, 7 days per week.

Accessing EAP Services

Members may access the EAP by calling the toll-free Helpline number, using the iConnectYou App, or instant messaging with a work-life consultant through the online instant messaging system.

  • Helpline: 888-993-7650
  • Email: eap@deeroaks.com
  • EAP Website
  • Login/Register for Account
    • Company code: utdallas
    • Email address – personal or work
    • Password – create a personal password

AllOne Health EAP also offers monthly newsletters for employees and supervisors who subscribe to their email list online.

This program offers a wide variety of counseling, referrals, and consultation services which can be accessed 24/7, and are all designed to assist employees and their families in resolving work and life issues in order to live happier, healthier, more balanced lives. The program offers up to 6 free counseling visits and a multitude of work/life services.

All employees (regardless of benefits eligibility or enrollment) and their household members, and children away at college may access the EAP services. This includes all graduate student employees (RAs, TAs and GAs).

Retirees and separated employees may access services within 6 months of their date of retirement or separation from the University.

AllOne Health Employee Assistance Program Services

Employee Support Services

EAP counselors support employees and their family/household members on a wide range of mental health, personal and work-related issues for which short-term counseling is appropriate, including but not limited to:

  • Anxiety/Depression
  • Family and Parenting Concerns
  • Stress/Tension
  • Caring for persons with disabilities
  • Coping with Grief and Loss
  • Workplace Conflicts
  • Anger and Stress Management
  • Dealing with Major Life Transitions
  • Couples and Relationship Issues
  • Alcohol and Substance Abuse Problems
  • Childcare and Eldercare Issues
  • Other Health and Wellness Issues
  • Online WILL Site Map

Work/Life Services

Today’s workforce manages many obligations in addition to work such as parenting, personal commitments, household responsibilities and other tasks that make life demanding. To help ease daily living stresses, the EAP offers a multitude of work/life services including Legal and Financial guidance, Child and Elder Care information, Concierge Services, a Safe Ride Program, and various training options.

Employee Training and Skills Development

Employees have unlimited access to upcoming and past online seminars by logging in to the EAP website .
