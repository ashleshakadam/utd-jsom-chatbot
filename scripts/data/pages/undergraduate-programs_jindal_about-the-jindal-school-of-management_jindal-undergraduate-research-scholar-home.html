    Boost Your Career Journey with Academic Research

    Engaging in research as an undergraduate student can have a profound impact on your overall academic performance, internship engagement and post-degree placement positions. Consulting, investment banking and analyst positions require strong analytical and research capabilities. Participating in research as an undergraduate student can help advance many aspects of your academic journey and beyond. Often students who become successful in research realize that their career may be pursuing a PhD and joining the academic ranks of a prominent university.

    Jindal Undergraduate Research Scholar (JURS) Program

    FALL COURSE:

    • Selected JURS students participate in a Welcome Seminar

    SPRING COURSE:

    • Defining Research for Knowledge and Innovation (BA3350)
    • Students submit a review paper and present it by the end of the semester
    • Students also submit papers if qualified for essay competitions
    • Summer Apprenticeship Research URAP and presentation

    FALL COURSE:

    • Managing Sustainable Operations (OPRE 3370/ITSS 3370)

      • OR

    • Independent research study with a faculty member

      • OR

    • Advanced Research Course (BA 4V91)
    • Students learn the emerging intersection of circular and digital aspects within the economy.
    • Student project presentations expected by the end of the semester

    SPRING COURSE:

    • Value Chain and Sustainability Practicum (OST Faculty Led Tour)

      • OR

    • Independent research study with a faculty member
    • Student reports and presentations are expected by the end of the semester
    • Summer Apprenticeship Research URAP and presentation

    FALL AND SPRING:

    • Participate in research and essay competitions approved by the Director, UG Research

    • Explore research career opportunities within academia and industries
    • Summer Apprenticeship Research URAP and presentation
    • Participate in the Wege Prize Research competition or others, if approved by the Director, UG Research (Can replace Senior Capstone course, need prior approval by Asst. Dean, Senior Capstone Projects) OR continue independent study with a faculty member
    • Students who continue to be in the program for the full four years will receive the âCertificate of Completion in JURSâ and can receive recommendation letters from mentoring faculty members
    • Students who publish peer-reviewed journal papers from their research before they graduate will be awarded the âCertificate of Excellence in JURSâ from the Dean and can receive recommendation letters from mentoring faculty members

    Excellent Returns for your Research Investment

    Successful research students will earn coveted recommendation letters from faculty that can be used for employment purposes or to pursue PhD programs.

    Funding and Scholarship Opportunities

    Admitted students will be funded for travel and accommodation expenses to attend conferences, get feedback from industry and academic experts and publish in peer-reviewed journals.

    UT Dallas offers several opportunities for undergraduate students to present their work in UG research poster competitions, the Annual Sustainability Conference, get selected to the coveted Green Fellowship Program at UT Southwestern, etc.
