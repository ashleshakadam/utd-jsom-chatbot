    Fintech & Digital Assets Workshop 2025 (UTD COMTECH)

    Organized By Center for the Management of Financial and Digital Asset Technologies

    February 21, 2025


    The Naveen Jindal School of Management at The University of Texas at Dallas is proud to host the second biennial UT Dallas Fintech and Digital Assets Workshop 2025 (UTD COMTECH) on February 21, 2025.

    The convergence of digital technologies, policy initiatives, regulations, and the disruption of financial services brought about by new Information Technologies has given impetus to the new FD-Tech Center in the Naveen Jindal School of Management at UT Dallas.

    Workshop Highlights:

    • In-depth insights into the latest trends in Fintech
    • Strategies for digital transformation in finance
    • Case studies and success stories
    • Networking opportunities with industry professionals
    • Q&A session with the keynote speaker
