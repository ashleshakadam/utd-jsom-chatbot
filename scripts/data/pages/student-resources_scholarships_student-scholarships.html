    Fast-track Jindal School students in the marketing program with an analytics or customer insights focus are eligible for semesters during which they are enrolled in graduate-level courses. GR

UG, GR

    This scholarship provides support for Jindal School students engaged in the campus community. Applicants must be full-time Jindal School students, have at least a 3.5 cumulative GPA and be engaged with the UT Dallas campus community, such as with UT Dallas student organizations, student government, campus volunteerism and/or campus employment.

    Preference may be given to students who are active in the Financial Leadership Association and/or have contributed to the success of the UTD SOM Investment Corporation. UG, GR

    This scholarship was established by the Chalak Mitra Group, founded by alumnus Ron Parikh BS01, in support of the Naveen Jindal School of Management undergraduate students.     Charles Quinn EMBA08 and his wife, Christina BS12, established this award to honor those who have served in the U.S. military. Quinn is a member of the Jindal School Advisory Council. Requirements include applicants must be full-time undergraduate students, have a 3.0 minimum cumulative GPA at UT Dallas and be either U.S. citizens or U.S. permanent residents.

    Preference will be given to U.S. veterans who earned a campaign medal for overseas service or served in one of the following: USA Special Forces or Rangers, USAF Special Operations, USMC Force Reconnaissance, USN Special Warfare or Combatant. UG

    This scholarship was established by alumnus Sunny Sun MS10, a professor in entrepreneurship and innovation, in support of Jindal School students. Applicants must be full-time Jindal School students, and preference may be given to applicants who have completed all or part of their preparatory education in China, Hong Kong, Macau or Taiwan; or are active in UT Dallas programs or clubs that promote entrepreneurial activities. UG, GR

Applicants must have a 3.0 minimum cumulative GPA at UT Dallas and, among equally qualified students, have completed all or part of their secondary education in China. UG, GR

Applicants must be full-time Jindal School students, have completed or are enrolled in at least one real estate course and have a 3.0 minimum cumulative GPA at UT Dallas. UG, GR

    The CVS Health Foundation provides scholarships to those interested in the intersection of healthcare and retail management. UG

Requirements include applicants must be full-time graduate students who have completed at least 9 hours in the Jindal School and have a 3.25 minimum cumulative GPA at UT Dallas. Applicants must be engaged on campus through a student organization, student government, campus volunteerism and/or campus employment. Preference may be for students who have had preparatory education in India. GR

Applicants must be U.S. citizens or U.S. permanent residents who graduated from a Texas high school and have a 3.0 minimum cumulative GPA at UT Dallas.

    Applicants must have a FAFSA on file at UT Dallas. This scholarship is renewable if the recipient continues to demonstrate leadership and remains academically eligible. (Recipient must re-apply each year.) UG

    Preference may be given to applicants who

    1. Have completed all or part of their preparatory education outside of the United States;
    2. Have made a positive impact on healthcare;
    3. Are enrolled in or plan to enroll in UT Dallas courses that focus on improving world healthcare; or
    4. Have overcome significant financial barriers in pursuit of his/her education.
